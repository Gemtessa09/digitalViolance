Essential Safety Tips

Use Strong, Unique Passwords

Create passwords with at least 12 characters including uppercase, lowercase, numbers, and symbols. Use a password manager to generate and store unique passwords for every account.

Watch tutorial

Enable Two-Factor Authentication

Add an extra layer of security by enabling 2FA on all important accounts. Use authenticator apps instead of SMS for better security.

Learn more on CISA.gov

Recognize Phishing Attempts

Never click suspicious links or download attachments from unknown senders. Check sender email addresses carefully and look for spelling errors in messages.

Phishing detection guide

Block and Report Harassment

Don't engage with harassers. Use platform blocking features immediately and report abusive behavior to platform moderators and authorities.

Report cyber harassment
